Classroom

Learning should not stop during holidays. It offers a robust video conferencing

solution to stimulate a virtual classroom experience to students, thus facilitating distance learning. Educators can broadcast their video feed along with their whiteboard in real time to the audience. Viewers can raise questions, share their screens and engage with other participants, bridging the gap between educators and students, regardless of their location.

Plagiarism Detection

Do away with the teachers’ workload crisis. Chalkkeeper has an integrated plagiarism
